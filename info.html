Basics:

This is a weekly tournament starting on May 30th. You will receive points every week based on how you place, so make sure to show up as much as you can!


There are 2 brackets each week, but only the singles bracket will count for points. The special bracket is just for fun.

At the end of the season, there will be a bracket/event to finish the season. This will be seeded based on your ranking. If 16 or more people have points at the end of the season, the top 8 will be placed in a bracket. If less than 16 people have participated at some point in the season then the top 4 will do a round-robin.

Note: No matter what format the finals is, the entire thing will be best of 5.

IN THE CASE OF A ROUND-ROBIN:

After everyone plays each other, the top two will then play for the finals of the entire season.
