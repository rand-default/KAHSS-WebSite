Basics:

This is a weekly event starting on May 30th. You will receive points every week based on how you place, so make sure to show up as much as you can and try your hardest!


There are 2 brackets each week, but only the singles bracket will count for points. The special bracket is just for fun.

At the end of the season, there will be a playoffs bracket to finish the season. This will be seeded based on your ranking. In the playoffs bracket half of the league participants will start in winners side and the other half will start in losers side. This provides a reason to try your hardest during the season, as only participants that rank in the top half will start in winners side.

PLAYOFFS BRACKET ELIGIBILITY:

To be eligible to participate in the playoffs bracket you must participate for at least 4 weeks of the league. This is so people who didnt participate for at least 4 weeks can not just come in at the end to farm out the reward.

During the regular season all matches will be best of 3 until top 3 in the bracket. That means winners finals, losers finals, and grand finals will all be best of 5.

During the playoffs bracket all sets will be best of 5.

SINGLES BRACKET RULESET:

-Ruleset: 3 Stock
-Time Limit: 7 minutes
-All items are turned off
-Stage Hazard Toggle: Off

BANS:

Bans are in a 1-2-1 format. For game 1 the lower ranked player gets to pick, which means they ban first. After game 1 the loser of the previous match bans first and picks the stage.
